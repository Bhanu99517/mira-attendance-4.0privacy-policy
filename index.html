Privacy & Data Policy

Official guidelines for Mira Attendance 4.0. We ensure your data is processed with transparency and high-level security.

Updated: May 20, 2024 Ver: 4.1.2
1. Information We Collect 2. How We Use Your Info 3. Cookies 4. Data Sharing 5. Data Security 6. User Rights 7. Contact Information

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide attendance and authentication services. 1.1 Personal Information Full name, Email address, Phone number, Employee / Student ID, Profile photograph. 1.2 Attendance Data Check-in/out timestamps, logs, and department details. 1.3 Biometric Data Facial feature data is converted into encrypted numerical templates. Raw images are not shared with third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to: Register and manage accounts, Record and verify attendance, Authenticate users securely, and improve system accuracy. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or biometric data.

3. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve user experience, analyze traffic, and maintain session security. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may be affected.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share data with trusted cloud providers or when required by law. All third parties must adhere to our strict data protection standards.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption, access controls, and secure servers. While no system is 100% secure, we use rigorous monitoring to protect your information.

6. User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data. You may withdraw consent for biometric processing at any time.
